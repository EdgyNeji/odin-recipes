Three Cheese Grilled Cheese Sandwich

This is a cult classic of a snack! Whether you're short on time or simply craving something delicious! And the best part? You only need a few ingredients: Your choice of three different cheeses, bread, and a small amount of butter!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • One tbs of butter
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Goat Cheese
  • American Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Add the cheese to one slice of bread.
  2. Lay the other slice of bread on top.
  3. Add the butter to a pan and set it on a low heat.
  4. Add the sandwich to the pan, cooking for 2 minutes on each side
  5. (Optional) Cook for longer if you want toastier bread!
