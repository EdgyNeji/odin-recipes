Keto Pancakes

The Healthier Breakfast Alternative

Looking for a "low carb" solution to your flapjack cravings? Well look no further!

Ingredients

  • Baking Powder
  • Cream Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Healthy Sweetener, like Monk Fruit

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs until uniform and fluffy.
  2. Add in the rest of the ingredients to the eggs.
  3. Cook in a pan on medium heat.
  4. They're ready to flip once bubbles appear on the surface.
  5. Enjoy with butter or healthy syrup alternatives!
